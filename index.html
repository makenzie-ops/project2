Akan Names

The Akan is a unique group of people who have dominated the ivory coast and ghana regions of West Africa.They have many sub-groups like;Fanti,Ashanti and Wassa.

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born.

Do you want to find out Akan name?

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week the were born and then depending on their gender, outputs the Akan name.
